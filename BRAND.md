## 1. Core positioning

### WHY — La creencia

Technology changed who gets to build.

A single person can now launch products, validate ideas, create digital businesses, publish MVPs, and generate income online — iterating quickly, independently. Artificial intelligence accelerated that shift.

We believe every person has ideas that can change their life and others'. We believe the world is better when people can choose their work, build what they imagine, generate their own income, and live on their own terms — from anywhere.

For decades, turning an idea into a real digital product required years of technical study or depending on others. AI changed that permanently.

### HOW — El sistema

A practical system that uses AI as its main development tool. No programming required, no code to write. Guided by someone with 20 years of building digital products, teaching from practice, without unnecessary theory.

### WHAT — El producto

A practical course where anyone can create and launch their first MVP using artificial intelligence. A real digital product, with user login and the ability to charge — ready to validate an idea with the market. With the tools to keep building independently, without depending on anyone.

### The core value

**construcción = independencia**

Building is not a technical skill. It is a capability that changes how you relate to work, business, and creativity. The student stops being someone who has ideas and starts being someone who executes them.

## 2. Buyer personas

Three audience types. Each has a different entry point, a different tension, and a different moment of breakthrough. Speak to one at a time — not all three at once.

### El Fundador

Tiene una idea concreta que lleva tiempo en su cabeza. Ha intentado contratar un desarrollador, buscar un socio técnico, o aprender a programar — y algo falló en cada intento. Su dolor es la brecha entre la idea y la realidad. Urgencia alta: la idea lo persigue.

**Momento liberador:** ver su idea funcionando en pantalla por primera vez, construida por él mismo.
**Resultado que busca:** su idea convertida en MVP validado, construido por él, sin depender de nadie.
**Transformación:** de la frustración de depender de otros a la autonomía de ejecutar solo.

### El Explorador

Es una persona independiente que quiere generar ingresos propios, sin depender de un empleador.

Ya probó algunos caminos previamente (pueden ser cosas como dropshipping, trading, inversión, o algun otro proyecto que no llegó a ningún lado).

Consume contenido sobre emprendedorismo. Es hábil con la tecnología y las herramientas digitales. 

Sabe que algo está cambiando y quiere subirlse a la ola de la innovación y la IA, y se pregunta cómo generar ingresos con ella.

Tiene motivación y acceso a información. Su dolor es no haber encontrado todavía el modelo que combine su independencia, la IA, y un negocio propio real. Tiene energía para intentarlo de nuevo.

No tiene paciencia para teoría — quiere ver el camino real antes de entrar.

**Momento liberador:** entender que con IA puede construir un producto digital propio, sin depender de nadie.
**Resultado que busca:** una fuente de ingresos propia y replicable, construida sobre algo que él creó.
**Transformación:**de probar modelos ajenos a construir el suyo. De buscar el próximo método a tener el sistema para crear sus propias oportunidades.

### El Empleado

Tiene trabajo estable, quizás bien pago. Pero construye para otros, bajo las reglas de otros, en los horarios de otros. Su dolor es el estancamiento: intercambia tiempo por dinero sin construir nada propio. Necesita sentir que el riesgo es bajo y que puede empezar en paralelo sin abandonar lo que tiene. Urgencia baja a media.

**Momento liberador:** lanzar su primer MVP mientras todavía tiene el trabajo que quiere dejar.
**Resultado que busca:** un producto propio funcionando en paralelo — una fuente de ingresos alternativa que le dé la seguridad suficiente para dar el salto cuando esté listo.
**Transformación:** de construir para otros a construir para sí mismo. De esperar el momento perfecto a tener algo concreto del otro lado.

## 3. Voice — four characteristics

The energy comes from **clarity, not volume**.

### 3.1 Directo
No detours, no inflated language. If it can be said with less, say it with less. Cut adjectives that don't carry information. One idea per sentence when possible.

### 3.2 Cercano
Someone with experience speaking peer-to-peer, not from a pedestal. Spanish neutral LATAM, **voseo natural**: *construís, podés, tenés, sabés*. No regional slang that excludes anyone.

### 3.3 Honesto
Don't promise what can't be delivered. Don't exaggerate outcomes. If something takes 6 weeks, say 6 weeks — not "in record time". If results vary, acknowledge it.

### 3.4 Motivador sin gritar
Clarity motivates. Empty epicness doesn't. Replace exclamation marks with concrete claims. Replace "increíble" with the actual thing that is increíble.

---

## 4. Language

- **Spanish neutral LATAM** with voseo (Argentine baseline, comprehensible across the region).
- Verb forms: *construís, podés, tenés, sabés, hacés, empezás* — never *construyes, puedes, tienes*.
- Avoid: *vos sos un crack*, *posta*, *re copado*, regional fillers that scope the audience down.
- Avoid English mixing unless the term is genuinely a term-of-art (*MVP, brief, prompt, deploy* are fine; *workflow* > *flujo de trabajo* is fine; *empoderarte* is not).
- Punctuation: prefer the period to the exclamation mark. Avoid abusing Em dashes `—` for parenthesis, makes the copy look AI written.

## 5. The five pillars

Every claim should map to one of these. If a piece of copy doesn't map cleanly, it's probably off-brand.

| Pilar | Qué significa |

## 7. Educational philosophy

The way the brand teaches is as important as what it teaches.

### Construcción primero
People learn faster when they create something tangible. Every module should move the student closer to a published page, a working MVP, a real workflow, a validated idea, a visible result.

### Claridad a través de la ejecución
Ideas become clearer while building. The process itself creates feedback, understanding, confidence, and direction — and reveals the next step. Clarity is not a prerequisite for starting; it is an outcome of starting.

### Simplicidad radical
Momentum matters. The experience should feel focused, lightweight, approachable, practical. A small number of tools. Clear steps. Fast iterations. Complexity is the enemy of execution.

### Validación temprana
A product becomes valuable when real people interact with it. Teach: launching early, capturing feedback, testing assumptions, learning from the market. Waiting for the perfect version is the same as not building.

### Independencia creativa
The ultimate outcome is autonomy. The student gains the ability to explore ideas freely, prototype quickly, launch projects independently, and create opportunities for themselves. That capability changes how they relate to work, business, and creativity.

### Criterio estratégico
AI accelerates execution. Judgment defines direction. The difference between someone using tools and someone building products is the quality of their decisions: what to build, what to cut, when to launch. The brand should consistently teach decision-making, not only mechanics.

### Momentum como ventaja
Fast execution creates momentum: faster feedback, faster learning, faster iteration. Reducing friction matters more than maximizing perfection.

### Launch culture
Launching is part of the learning process. Publishing early accelerates clarity, validation, and product evolution. Building privately forever creates stagnation.

## 10. The role of AI

Artificial intelligence expands the execution capacity of individuals. Its role is to accelerate creation, reduce friction, and enable independent execution.

AI amplifies output. The student provides direction. The quality of what gets built depends on the quality of the thinking behind it.

The long-term advantage comes from judgment, creativity, taste, experience, and strategic thinking — not from access to tools. As AI becomes universal, critical thinking becomes more valuable, not less. The ability to decide what deserves to exist is one of the most important skills of this era. People who combine clear thinking with fast execution create disproportionate value.

## 11. Voice in practice — two examples

### Evitar (don't)

> "¡Este increíble curso revolucionario te dará todas las herramientas para triunfar en el mundo digital!"

Empty motivation, hype, stacked adjectives. Noise where there should be precision.

### Así sí (do)

> En 6 semanas, construís tu primer producto digital. Sin saber programar. Con una sola herramienta.

Concrete, temporal, honest. Says what happens, when, and what isn't required.

**The rewrite test:** strip every word that isn't load-bearing. If the sentence still says the same thing, ship the stripped version.

**The emotional target:** the reader should feel *"Ahora entiendo cómo transformar una idea en algo real"* — and later *"Puedo construir mucho más de lo que imaginaba."* Reach for clarity and possibility, not hype.

## 12. Audience

The reader is a founder, creator, or freelancer with an idea and no way to execute it alone. They're not a coder and don't aspire to be one. They aspire to autonomy: building and owning something real, on their own terms.

Speak to them as a peer who has already crossed the bridge. Not as a teacher. Not as a guru. Not as a "growth hacker."

For detailed persona breakdowns, see §2.

## 13. Hard "no"s

Never write any of these:

- **Hype motivacional.** *increíble · revolucionario · cambiará tu vida · el secreto · al fin · finalmente.*
- **Generic tech promises.** *Domina · todas las herramientas · el mundo digital · el futuro es ahora.*
- **Stacked adjectives.** *innovador, disruptivo, transformador.* Pick zero.
- **Empty CTAs.** *Sumate · descubrilo · animate.* Use concrete verbs: *Empezá módulo · Ver sílabo · Inscribirme.*
- **Hacker / Web3 register.** *unlock · ship faster · 10x · matrix · cripto · web3.* No.
- **"Hacete rico con IA."** The course is about building real products, not about getting rich quick. No income promises, no lifestyle fantasy.
- **Humo.** No vague promises, no magical outcomes, no testimonials without context.
- **Ariel's photo as brand face.** Don't use Ariel's photo as a central or recurring element in communication. Photography appears only when context explicitly warrants it: when presenting him as a mentor, showing his CV or background, or when the content names him directly. The emphasis is on the message and the student, not on the creator.

## 14. Tone by surface

| Surface | Tone | Length |
|---|---|---|
| Hero / landing headline | Declarative, present tense, voseo, prefer a WHY + HOW format | ≤ 14 words |
| Subhead / lede | Concrete promise + scope, here is the room for the WHAT | 1–2 sentences |
| Module title | Noun phrase or imperative | ≤ 6 words |
| Module description | What gets built · what's the entregable | 1–2 sentences |
| Body / explanation | Peer-to-peer, paragraphs ≤ 4 sentences | as needed |
| CTA | Imperative verb + object | 2–4 words |
| Toast / banner | Plain present, action implied | 1 short sentence + helper line |
| Form error | Specific cause, specific fix | 1 sentence |

## 15. Quick checklist before shipping copy

1. Does it map to one of the five pillars?
2. Is every adjective load-bearing? Cut the rest.
3. Is the verb in voseo where applicable?
4. Did I avoid hype words from §13?
5. If it's a CTA, is it a concrete verb on a concrete noun?
6. Could a peer say this out loud without cringing? If not, rewrite.
7. Does it speak to one of the three personas (§2) without alienating the other two?
8. Does it teach a way of thinking, not only a tool?
9. Does it encourage execution or paralysis?

## 16. Long-term brand direction

The brand evolves toward founder education, independent product creation, and AI-assisted entrepreneurship — practical systems for people who want to build under their own terms.

The ability to build independently becomes more valuable every year. The brand should grow with that shift by consistently helping people move from ideas to products, from hesitation to execution, from dependence to autonomy.
