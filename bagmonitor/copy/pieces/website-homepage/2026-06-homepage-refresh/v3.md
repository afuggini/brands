## The Platform

**Eyebrow:** The Platform

**Heading:** Three pillars. **One platform.**

**Subheading:**
The infrastructure layer that turns compliance obligations into operational intelligence — and positions your airline for the next decade of passenger growth.

**Features:**

### 01 — Smart Tags
Airline-grade physical tags that give every bag a persistent digital identity from check-in to destination — the physical foundation for IATA Resolution 753 compliance and beyond.

- Serialized tracking ID
- Airline branding capable
- Weather resistant

### 02 — Real-Time Monitoring
Live dashboards, transfer risk alerts, exception detection, and SLA monitoring. Converts the four mandatory R753 checkpoints into continuous operational intelligence across the full baggage lifecycle.

- Live movement map
- Delay detection
- Escalation workflows

### 03 — Enterprise API
REST APIs, webhooks, and real-time event streams. Integrates with DCS, BRS, and your existing airline systems — and aligns with IATA Modern Baggage Messaging (MBM) v2, ratified in 2025.

- REST & webhooks
- Event streams
- Multi-tenant RBAC
