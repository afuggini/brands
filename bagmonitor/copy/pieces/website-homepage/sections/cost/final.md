# The Cost of No Visibility — Final

**Eyebrow:** The Cost

**Heading:** The Cost of Lost Visibility

**Intro:**
The airline industry spends approximately $5 billion annually on mishandled baggage — 33.4 million events in 2024 alone. A single mishandling event generates three separate, compounding costs.

---

**Operational cost**
~$150 per event in courier, staff, and claims costs - before the bag is located, before tracing starts, before any claim is filed.

**Recovery overhead**
Additional ~$22 per WorldTracer activation for searching for a bag whose location is unknown.

**Compensation exposure**
Up to ~$2,091 per passenger internationally and $4,700 on US domestic routes on every mishandled bag that generates a claim, including the 74% classified as delays.

**Passenger trust**
Mishandling negatively impacts NPS score, with 47% of passengers saying real-time tracking increases their confidence in checking a bag.
