# The Cost of No Visibility — v7

**Eyebrow:** The Cost

**Heading:** The Cost of Lost Visibility

**Intro:**
The airline industry spends approximately $5 billion annually on mishandled baggage. A single event generates three separate cost layers — most carriers track only the first.

---

**Operational cost**
The floor is ~$150 per event — courier transport, staff hours, and claims overhead — incurred before the bag's location is even known.

**Recovery overhead**
Add $22 per WorldTracer activation when the bag's location is unknown — a conditional cost that applies on top of the operational baseline.

**Compensation exposure**
Add liability of up to ~$2,091 per passenger on international routes and $4,700 on US domestic routes — applied to all mishandled bags, including the 74% that are delays rather than permanent losses.

**Passenger trust**
47% of passengers say real-time tracking would increase their confidence in checking a bag; those who don't experience it check fewer bags, choose different carriers, and file claims more aggressively.
