# The Cost of No Visibility — v8

**Eyebrow:** The Cost

**Heading:** The Cost of Lost Visibility

**Intro:**
The airline industry spends approximately $5 billion annually on mishandled baggage — 33.4 million events in 2024 alone. Three of those cost layers are preventable. One is structural.

---

**Operational cost**
~$150 per event in courier logistics, staff time, and claims overhead is the cost of a failure that real-time visibility would have prevented.

**Recovery overhead**
The $22 WorldTracer activation fee is the cost of not knowing where a bag is — a cost that doesn't exist when the bag's location is tracked continuously.

**Compensation exposure**
Liability of up to ~$2,091 per passenger internationally and $4,700 on US domestic routes applies to every passenger who files a claim — including 74% of events classified as delays, not permanent losses.

**Passenger trust**
Real-time tracking increases passenger confidence in checking a bag for 47% of travelers — and airlines that can't provide it absorb the difference in lower check-in rates and more escalated claims.
