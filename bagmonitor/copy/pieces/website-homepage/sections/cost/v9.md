# The Cost of No Visibility — v9

**Eyebrow:** The Cost

**Heading:** The Cost of Lost Visibility

**Intro:**
$5 billion per year. 33.4 million mishandling events in 2024. Three cost layers. Most carriers track only one.

---

**Operational cost**
~$150 per event in courier, staff, and claims costs — before the bag is located, before tracing starts, before any claim is filed.

**Recovery overhead**
$22 per WorldTracer activation — the minimum cost of searching for a bag whose location should already be known.

**Compensation exposure**
Up to ~$2,091 per passenger internationally and $4,700 on US domestic routes — on every mishandled bag that generates a claim, including the 74% classified as delays.

**Passenger trust**
47% of passengers say real-time tracking increases their confidence in checking a bag — those who don't get it check fewer bags, escalate claims, and choose different carriers next time.
