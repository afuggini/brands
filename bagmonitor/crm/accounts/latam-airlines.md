Incorporaron realidad aumentada para saber si el equipaje ya había venido roto y no pagarlo. Con el de control de apertura van a poder determinar el lugar donde se realizó la apertura."*
- **What LATAM is building:** AR system to document bag condition at check-in to deny liability for pre-existing damage. They need a complementary solution to determine *where* bag opening/tampering occurred in the chain

---

## Key Insight

LATAM's problem is accountability, not tracking. They want to prove at which point in the handler chain a bag was opened or tampered with. BagMonitor's opening detection (if/when developed) maps directly to this. The tracking use case was not their pain.
