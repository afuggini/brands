## Context

- **Scale (local):** 11,000 passengers, 13,000 bags/month. 209 airports globally
- **Tech stack:** SITA Bag Manager — 6 scanners per flight + per-piece charge (~$0.075/bag). Full IATA R753 compliance. iPads at arrivals for pre-landing alerts
- **Cargo:** Dedicated freighters (29 pallets, 100 tons) + belly cargo. VAL (high-value cargo) >$1,000/kg: phones, gold, metals, computers. Systematic theft by Intercargo ramp staff — minimum 1 phone/week
- **Duty free theft:** Crew stealing AirPods (€379/unit) from onboard duty free
- **Decision structure:** Diego is AOC President (industry body), not Lufthansa commercial. Maximiliano is the Cargo Manager — actual decision-maker for cargo tracking
- **Local note:** New AA2000 contract includes SITA Bag Manager upgrade — already in the pipeline for passenger baggage
