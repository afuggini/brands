## Context

- **Scale:** 150M passengers/year, 900 aircraft, 135 countries, 18 baggage belts at Istanbul
- **DCS:** Quick (check-in), linked to SITA
- **Handler:** Fully outsourced locally (Ross Racer handles baggage in Buenos Aires); 10 own employees in Argentina
- **Star Alliance:** Joint liability with codeshare partners (e.g. United) for through-check baggage
- **Operations note:** Will sacrifice baggage for on-time departure (slot); notify passenger on arrival
- **Pain acknowledged:** World Tracer cost ~$22/transaction. Cost pressure from NDC transition
- **Regulatory:** Aware of and tracking IATA R753 compliance

---

## Key Objections

1. **Scale**: Turkish implements systems globally or not at all — no phased rollouts by route
2. **Outsourced operations**: All ground handling is third-party; change management is complex
3. **Decision location**: All decisions made in Istanbul. Buenos Aires is a relay only
