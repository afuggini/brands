# Mission, Vision & Thesis

## Mission

To eliminate baggage visibility gaps across the global aviation ecosystem.

## Vision

A world where every piece of baggage has a complete, real-time digital identity from check-in to destination — and where airlines and operators have the intelligence to act before problems occur.

## Core Thesis

> Lost baggage is not a passenger problem. It is an infrastructure visibility problem.

BagMonitor provides end-to-end traceability, monitoring, and operational intelligence across the baggage lifecycle.

## Why Now

The timing for BagMonitor is structurally strong. Global air passenger traffic hit a record 5.3 billion in 2024 and is projected to reach 8 billion by 2037 (IATA). Baggage volumes are scaling proportionally, yet the industry still mishandles 33.4 million bags per year at a cost of approximately $5 billion annually. The industry has demonstrated that tech investment reduces mishandling rates — but most airlines still lack comprehensive real-time visibility infrastructure. By 2027, 82% of airlines plan to offer passengers real-time bag tracking. The infrastructure to deliver that does not yet exist at scale. BagMonitor is built to fill that gap.
