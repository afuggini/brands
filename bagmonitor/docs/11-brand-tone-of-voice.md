# Brand & Tone of Voice

## Positioning Statement

BagMonitor is **baggage visibility infrastructure** — not a gadget brand, not a passenger app.

> Visual identity and the design system live in the brand layer, not in docs: see `../brand/DESIGN.md` (v1) and `../v2/DESIGN.md` (current).

## Tone of Voice

**Use:**
- Confident
- Technical
- Clear
- Infrastructure-focused
- Enterprise-ready
- Data-driven

**Avoid:**
- Consumer tone
- Overly marketing-heavy or hype language
- Buzzword inflation
- First-person "we" before establishing credibility

## Messaging Hierarchy

1. Infrastructure / reliability
2. Integration capability
3. Operational ROI
4. Passenger trust improvement (secondary benefit, not lead message)
