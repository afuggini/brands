## Roadmap Informed by Industry Direction (SITA 2025)

The following trends from SITA's 2025 report should directly inform BagMonitor's product roadmap priorities:

- **AI for baggage operations:** 40% of airports are already using AI for real-time congestion management. AI-powered exception prediction is a logical next layer for BagMonitor's platform.
- **Biometric-enabled bag drop:** 19% of airports have implemented biometric bag drop; 46% plan to by 2027. BagMonitor should consider hooks for biometric workflow integration.
- **Off-airport and remote check-in:** Early examples (e.g., Red Sea International Airport / DUBZ hotel check-in) show baggage journeys starting outside the airport. BagMonitor's tracking should be extensible to off-airport check-in triggers.
- **MBM v2 standard:** The industry ratified Modern Baggage Messaging Version 2 in 2025. Compliance with this standard is expected to reduce mishandling by 5% through data quality improvements. BagMonitor's messaging architecture should align with or natively support MBM v2.
