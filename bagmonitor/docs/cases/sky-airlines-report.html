How It Works

No gates. No scanners. No infrastructure.

BagMonitor is built for carriers that need real-time baggage visibility without the capital expense of fixed RFID infrastructure.

01
Smart tag at check-in
A small wireless tag (30mm × 5mm) is attached to each bag at check-in and linked to the passenger's booking. No passenger action required.
02
Staff smartphones as nodes
Any staff member running the BagMonitor app on their phone automatically detects every tagged bag within 100 meters. No dedicated scanners or fixed infrastructure needed.
03
Continuous real-time location
Unlike checkpoint-based systems, BagMonitor knows where each bag is at all times — at the ramp, in transfer, at loading — not just at mandatory scan points.
04
Integrates with your existing stack
BagMonitor connects to Sabre, Amadeus, and other DCS systems via API. Automatic reconciliation cross-references bag location against the flight manifest in real time.
