## One-Line Description

BagMonitor is an enterprise infrastructure company providing smart physical baggage tags, real-time tracking software, and enterprise APIs to airlines and transport operators.

## Elevator Pitch

Lost baggage is not a passenger experience problem — it is a visibility infrastructure failure. BagMonitor connects physical baggage identity with digital monitoring systems, giving airlines and ground operators end-to-end traceability, operational intelligence, and the tools to reduce cost, improve performance, and increase passenger trust at scale.
