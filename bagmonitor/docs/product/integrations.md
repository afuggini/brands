## IATA Resolution 753 Alignment

IATA Resolution 753 mandates airlines to track baggage at four key points:

1. Check-in / bag acceptance
2. Loading onto aircraft
3. Transfer between flights
4. Arrival / delivery to passenger

BagMonitor's scan-based tracking model maps directly to these four mandatory milestones and exceeds them by providing continuous monitoring and exception detection between those points. This is a compliance acceleration argument — not just a product benefit.
