The platform

Visibility, exception detection, and intelligence — across the lifecycle.

01

Real-time lifecycle tracking

A complete digital identity for every bag, updated at each handling point from check-in to claim. Augments your DCS and BRS through secure APIs — no rip-and-replace.

02

Transfer exception alerts

SLA monitoring and risk alerts fire at the transfer points where bags actually go missing — early enough for ground ops to re-route, re-tag, or hold a load.

03

Predictive risk scoring

Route analytics and bottleneck detection score transfer risk before a connection is missed, turning raw tracking data into operational decisions.

The thesis

Prevention over recovery.

Finding a lost bag faster is a better failure. The goal is to stop the bag from being lost — by catching the exception while it can still be acted on.
